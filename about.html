Who Are We?

The McKinnon Basketball Association is a not-for-profit community sporting association, providing people within the cities of Glen Eira, Bayside and Kingston the opportunity to play and be involved with great game of basketball.

The Association consists of seven domestic clubs (Jets, Lakers, Magic, Raptors, Saints, Scalpers and Wolves), competing throughout the week in both male and female competitions across a wide range of age groups. With over 250 teams competing, the domestic program continues to grow and improve.

The McKinnon Basketball Association has also established a strong representative program, with 32 teams competing against a range of clubs across Victoria this 2016 Season. The Cougars program has gone from strength to strength in recent years, with more and more teams competing at the highest level.

The MBA is an inclusive, community-focused and family-oriented Association, constantly striving to provide great opportunities for all members to better their basketball. Providing an introduction to basketball for the youngest through Aussie Hoops and Slam Jam, the MBA has provided a clear pathway for all juniors looking to play. McKinnon's All Abilities program has grown immensely, with the Superstars representing McKinnon in the VJBL's Victorian Inclusion League as well as various tournaments throughout the year.

The McKinnon Basketball Association also has four strong Big V teams who compete in one of the world's largest semi-professional basketball leagues. Both our Senior Mens and Womens teams compete in the State Championship Division, the highest in the league. Our Cougar Men competed in the 2015 Grand Final in just their second season in the State Championship division. Our Youth Women and Mens teams provide a strong link between our junior and senior competitions, with our Men competing in the Victorian Youth Championship division and our Women in Youth League One.
